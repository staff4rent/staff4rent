Job Details

Job Title: Waitstaff (Restaurants & Corporate Events)

Location: New York City Metro

Employment Type: Part-Time / Full-Time

Hourly Rate: $16 plus tips

Job Summary

We are seeking attentive and professional Waitstaff to provide exceptional dining service for restaurants, corporate events, and special occasions. As a member of our waitstaff team, you will play a vital role in ensuring guests have a memorable and seamless dining experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Set up and prepare tables, seating arrangements, and dining areas to event specifications.
  • Greet guests, take orders, and serve food and beverages in a timely, courteous manner.
  • Ensure that food presentation meets quality standards and handle special requests with professionalism.
  • Clear tables, clean dining areas, and assist with post-event clean-up.
  • Collaborate with kitchen staff to ensure efficient food service and timely delivery.
  • Maintain a high standard of cleanliness and adhere to all food safety regulations.

Qualifications

  • Previous experience in waitstaff, hospitality, or customer service roles preferred.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills with a focus on guest satisfaction.
  • Ability to work well in a team and adapt to a fast-paced environment.
  • Attention to detail, reliability, and a positive attitude.
  • Flexible schedule, with availability for weekends and evenings as needed.

Benefits

  • Competitive pay and flexible shift options.
  • Opportunities to work at high-profile events and exclusive venues.
  • Training and skill development to advance within the hospitality industry.
  • Friendly, team-oriented work environment.
