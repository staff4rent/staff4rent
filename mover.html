Job Details

Job Title: Residential and Commercial Mover

Location: New York City Metro

Employment Type: Part-Time / Full-Time

Hourly Rate: Starting at $18

Job Summary

We are looking for reliable and strong movers to join our team in providing top-quality moving services for both residential and commercial clients. As a mover, you will handle packing, loading, transporting, and unloading of items, ensuring that each move is smooth, efficient, and handled with care. If you have a strong work ethic, attention to detail, and the physical ability to handle moving tasks, we’d love to have you on board!

Key Responsibilities

  • Carefully pack, load, and unload items, including furniture, appliances, and boxes, for residential and commercial moves.
  • Ensure the safe transport of client belongings by securely arranging items in the moving truck.
  • Disassemble and reassemble furniture as needed.
  • Communicate effectively with clients and team members to coordinate moves.
  • Follow safety guidelines to prevent damage to items and injury during the move.

Qualifications

  • Previous moving or warehouse experience is an advantage but not required.
  • Ability to lift heavy items and work on your feet for extended periods.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Good communication skills and a customer-friendly attitude.
  • Reliable transportation to job sites.

Benefits

  • Flexible working hours to fit your schedule.
  • Competitive pay with opportunities for advancement.
  • Paid training and support from experienced team members.
  • Supportive, team-oriented work environment.
