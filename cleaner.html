Job Title: Residential and Commercial Cleaner

Location: New York City Metro

Employment Type: Part-Time / Full-Time

Job Summary

We are seeking dedicated and meticulous cleaners to join our team for residential and commercial cleaning services. In this role, you will help maintain the cleanliness and organization of a variety of spaces, from family homes to office buildings. If you are a detail-oriented individual with a passion for creating spotless environments, this role is perfect for you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform routine cleaning tasks such as sweeping, mopping, dusting, and vacuuming in residential and commercial settings.
  • Sanitize bathrooms, kitchens, and high-contact surfaces to ensure a healthy environment.
  • Deep-clean carpets, windows, and hard floors as required.
  • Maintain an organized and safe cleaning environment.
  • Identify and report any maintenance needs or repairs.

Qualifications

  • Previous experience in residential or commercial cleaning is a plus but not required.
  • Strong attention to detail and commitment to delivering high-quality service.
  • Ability to work independently and manage time efficiently.
  • Good communication skills and professional demeanor.
  • Reliable transportation to various job sites.

Benefits

  • Flexible working hours to fit your schedule.
  • Competitive pay with room for growth.
  • Opportunities to increase hours based on performance.
  • Supportive and friendly work environment.
