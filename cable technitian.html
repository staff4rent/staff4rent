Job Details

Job Title: Residential and Commercial Cable Technician

Location: New York City Metro

Employment Type: Part-Time / Full-Time

Weekly Rate: Starting at $1200

Job Summary

We are seeking skilled Cable Technicians to join our team, serving both residential and commercial clients. As a Cable Technician, you will be responsible for installing, troubleshooting, and maintaining cable systems, ensuring each job is completed efficiently and meets high-quality standards. If you have technical expertise, a strong work ethic, and a passion for delivering excellent customer service, this role is for you!

Key Responsibilities

  • Install, maintain, and troubleshoot cable systems, including internet, TV, and phone lines, for both residential and commercial properties.
  • Conduct site surveys to assess the best locations for cables and equipment.
  • Test and inspect systems to confirm functionality and quality.
  • Provide clients with guidance on equipment operation and troubleshoot connectivity issues.
  • Work closely with the team and clients to complete installations safely and efficiently.

Qualifications

  • Previous experience as a Cable Technician or related field preferred.
  • Knowledge of cable systems, wiring, and signal strength testing.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Excellent customer service skills and ability to explain technical issues to clients.
  • Reliable transportation and the ability to travel to job sites.

Benefits

  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate your availability.
  • Competitive pay and advancement opportunities.
  • Paid training and development with support from an experienced team.
  • Friendly, supportive work environment.
