Job Details

Job Title: Professional Babysitter (Residential)

Location: New York City Metro

Employment Type: Part-Time / Full-Time

Hourly Rate: Starting at $18

Job Summary

We are looking for compassionate and experienced Nannies to provide high-quality childcare for families in residential settings. This role involves caring for children, supporting their development, and ensuring a safe and nurturing environment. If you have a love for working with children, strong communication skills, and a responsible nature, we encourage you to apply!

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide attentive care tailored to each child's needs, including meal preparation, activities, and bedtime routines.
  • Plan and engage in educational and recreational activities that support children's growth and development.
  • Maintain a safe and clean environment, ensuring children’s safety at all times.
  • Communicate regularly with parents to keep them informed about their children’s activities and well-being.
  • Assist with light household tasks related to childcare, such as laundry and tidying up play areas.

Qualifications

  • Previous childcare or nannying experience required.
  • Understanding of child development stages and needs.
  • Strong organizational skills, patience, and a nurturing approach.
  • Excellent communication skills and professionalism.
  • CPR and First Aid certifications preferred or willingness to obtain.

Benefits

  • Flexible work schedule with part-time and full-time options.
  • Competitive pay with opportunities for advancement.
  • Supportive and safe working environment.
  • Ongoing training and development opportunities.
